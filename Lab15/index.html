My hobbies are: Playing Basketball, Reading, Traveling...v.v But I like to play Basketball the most. I started playing in the 11th grade. At first, the practice was very difficult, my parents prevented me because I feared it would affect my education. But after a while, I tried to strike a balance between studying and playing basketball, and then my parents agreed to let me play. I used that loss as a motivation to continue practicing. And by the time I was 19, I had won the citywide U19 age championship. Basketball has given me a lot of things, how to stand up after failure, how to not give up no matter where you are.
